Our Mission

CampusFind is a centralized Digital Lost & Found portal designed to help university students reunite with their lost belongings quickly and efficiently.

How It Works

  1. Report: Students can report lost or found items with details and images
  2. Verify: Admin team reviews and verifies all submissions
  3. Browse: Students can search through verified items
  4. Coordinate: Security personnel help facilitate returns

How do I report a lost item?

Click on 'Login', select Student, then click 'Report' and fill in the item details.

How long does verification take?

Our admin team typically verifies items within 24 hours.

Can I edit my report after submission?

Please contact admin support for any changes to submitted reports.

How do I claim an item?

Contact the person who found the item using the contact information provided, or visit the Security office.

Who can access this platform?

All registered students, admin staff, and security personnel can access CampusFind.
